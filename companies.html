What can we do for your company?

Our standards when presenting shortlists to clients set us apart from our competition.



Our commitment to you:

  • Providing a tailor made 360 recruitment approach to your organization.
  • Once informed of the vacancy from you, we will strive to provide an assortment of candidates that match your requirements in an agreed timeframe. Candidates will be presented not only based on technical skills and ability to do the job, but also the cultural and personality fit required to function in your organization.
  • We screen all candidates before presenting them by asking relevant technical and behavioral questions. This includes a detailed career consultation, ensure their goals align with those of your organization. This method has proven to increase retention rates.
  • After a successful placement we maintain contact with both you and the candidate to ensure both parties are satisfied.
  • We will provide you with feedback and insight on the recruitment process.
